CAD Drafting

Here are a couple examples of some CAD draftings I made over the years. The main program I use is AutoCAD, and I'm familiar with both the 2D and 3D workspace, as well as plating and exporting.

My process involces first drafting what I can in 2D, then extruding bodies and shapes, assembling in 3D, and finally adding smaller details before plating and exporting.

For show-specific draftings, please find those files in their respective gallery pages.
