Into The Woods (2020)

Into The Woods was my last show in high school and the largest I have participated in. For this production, I was the sole lighting designer in charge of bringing each character to life on stage, with some help from our tech director, Brian Wedeking. Since the story revolves around various fairytales, I chose to use a combination of colors and effects to represent the mood of each character in a given scene. I made the cue sheets and called the cues during the show. I was also involved in building various set pieces, such as bookcases for the opening and Rapunzel's tower and coaching our Freshmen. Overall, I am very proud of my work during this show!

Role: Lighting Designer, Production Electrician, Carpenter
